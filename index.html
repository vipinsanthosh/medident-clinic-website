About Us

Experience Healthcare

Medident Health Clinic was commenced with avowed commitment towards ethics and excellence in health care. We are a medical ,dental and ayurveda clinic were a patient finds complete health care under one roof. Besides providing with sophisticated dental treatments by experienced doctors with the aid of state - of - the-art technological equipments we make available of ENT specialist, psychology consultation by most experienced specialists in the region. We treat our patients in a friendly and warm environment so as to make him feel fully comfortable and stress free.
      Our clinic follows strict standards of cleanliness and sterilization protocol for patients and doctor hygiene to nullify the possibility of any complication. Our clinic as well as follow strict covid-19 protocol.

Affordable care
We have always kept ethics over bussiness and have never been involved in unwarranted or unwanted treatment or practice and our charges are moderate.
Best and Latest Care
We have not sit on our laurels and are continuously attuned to the new advancements and changes in our field of concern.
